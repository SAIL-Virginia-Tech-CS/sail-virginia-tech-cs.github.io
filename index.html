    About Me

    I'm an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Virginia Tech, where I lead the SAIL (Society + AI & Language) Lab. My research lies at the intersection of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). My work aims to advance Computational Social Science by using computational linguistics to better understand how AI-mediated systems impact interactions across people and machines.
